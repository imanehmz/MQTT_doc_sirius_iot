why use MQTT

MQTT is a low network lightweight protocol based on TCP/IP, it is low on energy and advantageous especially for wireless devices it reduces the network bandwidth in communication and minimizes data packets

Description

For our summer internship project, we worked on different IoT solutions at Sirius NET. We have got to learn a lot and to practice our programming skills that we acquired at school or at clubs. This internship opened our eyes to the IoT field, with our supervisor Mr Amine Fellahi that has been a generous encyclopedia that didn’t hesitate to share with us his years of experience in the field. Our solution is based on a BCE GPS device, it is a device that offers many functionalities such as GPS, weather data and can also be linked to other sensors such as the car’s CAN. We worked on getting the data from the BCE device to the server and database using the MQTT protocol,and translating the payloads from their formats to human readable information.

margin & padding

to add a margin or padding to an element, simply add the m-(1...5) for margin or m-(1...5) for padding, to be more specific use the direction specified classes.

direction margin padding
all m-(1...5) p-(1...5)
top mt-(1...5) pt-(1...5)
left ml-(1...5) pl-(1...5)
right mr-(1...5) pr-(1...5)
bottom mb-(1...5) pb-(1...5)

<!-- gives the paragraph a margin of 2rem and padding of 1rem -->

<p class="m-2 p-1">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.</p>

<!-- gives the paragraph a margin-top of 2rem and padding-left of 3rem -->

<p class="mt-2 pl-3">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.</p>

gap

to add a gap between the grid's elements use the g-(1...5) class on the parent element.

Flexbox

just like the grid system the flexbox system is easy to use, start by applying the flex class to the parent element, by default the flex-direction is set to row, to change that add the flex-col class.

class what it does
flex display : flex
flex-col flex-direction: column
justify-center justify-content: center
space-between justify-content: space-between
align-center align-items: center
flex-rev-row flex-direction: row-reverse
flex-rev-col flex-direction: column-reverse

to verticaly center elements use the vertical-center class.

<button class="btn-primary hov-1">click me</button>

<button class="btn-primary hov-2">click me</button>
